What is Funngro?

Funngro is a platform that connects teens with real companies to work on real projects. This is your chance to learn new skills, gain valuable experience, and earn real money.

  • Real Projects: Work on real projects for real companies, not just theoretical assignments.
  • Flexible Schedule: Work on your own time, from anywhere.
  • Learn & Grow: Develop new skills and gain experience that will benefit you in your future career.
  • Earn Money: Get paid for your work and save for your future goals.

How it Works

The process is simple and straightforward:

  1. Sign Up: Create a free account on the Funngro platform.
  2. Browse Projects: Discover exciting projects that match your skills and interests.
  3. Apply: Apply for the projects that you're interested in.
  4. Get Approved: Companies will review your application and let you know if you're selected.
  5. Start Working: Begin work on your selected project and earn money.
